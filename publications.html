2024 · npj Advanced Manufacturing (Nature)

Polybutadiene structural effects on dynamic wetting

S. D. Raissi, M. A. Magaña, A. Contreras, S. Wei, B. Brettmann & J. Kalman

Investigates how polybutadiene binder structure governs the dynamic wetting and spreading of droplets impacting ammonium perchlorate — key to expanding the design space for binder-jetting additive manufacturing of composite propellants. Spreading scales with t1/2 in the inertia-dominated regime before transitioning to a viscous regime; branched polymer variants spread up to 25% more by area than linear variants.

  • My contribution: high-speed experimental data reduction and analysis in MATLAB
  • Reduced the droplet-impact image sequences (above) to measure spreading through impact and maximum spread
